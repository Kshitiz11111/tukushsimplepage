Scholarships at Oxford College


Oxford College provides scholarships under various heading that covers merit based scholarship and need-based scholarship. Merit based scholarships are provided to outstanding students and need based scholarships are for those students who belongs to minority castes and physically and economically challenged. Students who have topped the entrance examination and have been listed within the top 12 are awarded with 100% to 25% free ship according to their position. Students with 90% marks in School leaving Certificate are awarded with full scholarship. Distinction holder in +2 gets full scholarship for Bachelor Level.
